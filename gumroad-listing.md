### 🚀 Turn "I want to start a business" into an actual plan — in under 30 minutes.

This bundle includes **two interactive browser-based tools** designed specifically for young entrepreneurs. No app download needed — just open the file in any web browser and go!

---

**📦 Tool 1: The Business Builder**

A full step-by-step business planning tool that walks kids through:

- **Finding their idea** — Pick your passions, skills, and what you see people needing. The tool generates personalized business ideas based on your answers.
- **Knowing your customers** — Who will buy? How close are they? What can they afford?
- **Setting the right price** — Enter your costs, see a live break-even price, a sweet-spot suggestion, and a visual price tracker that shows whether your price is too low, just right, or too high.
- **Hitting your sales goal** — Set an earning goal and the tool tells you exactly how many items to make and sell.
- **Charity donation option** — Toggle on a donation percentage (5–25%) and the tool automatically adjusts your price recommendations and shows exactly how much you'll give vs. keep.

Kids can start from any section and jump around — it's not locked into one order. A live hub dashboard updates automatically as they fill things in.

---

**🎪 Tool 2: The Fair Builder**

Built specifically for kids setting up a table at a fair, market, or school event:

- Pick your passions, skills, and what's in demand at fairs to generate idea suggestions
- Use the **feasibility filter** — the tool crosses out ideas that don't fit your money or time situation
- Vote Yes / Maybe / No on each idea until one winner rises to the top
- **Budget calculator** with per-item OR per-batch toggle (great for baked goods and batch-made items)
- **Price decider** with break-even math, suggested prices, and a visual price tracker
- **Sales goal screen** with a "can I make enough to cover my costs?" check
- Generates a printable **Fair Plan Summary** at the end
